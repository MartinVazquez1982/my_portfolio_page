About me

I am a Systems Engineer, passionate about technology and committed to excellence in my field. As part of my undergraduate thesis, I developed a segmentation model for endosaccular devices used in the treatment of cerebral aneurysms, based on 3D medical imaging. This project represented an initial contribution to the development of segmentation methods aimed at improving the diagnosis and treatment of this medical condition. My professional goal is to create solutions that not only meet expectations but exceed them, leaving a positive and lasting impact on society and the world of technology.
